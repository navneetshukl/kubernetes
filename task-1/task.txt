## BEGINNER LEVEL (Setup & Basics)

**Goal:** Understand Pods, Deployments, and Services

1. **Run a Golang Hello World App**

   * Learn how to deploy a simple container to Kubernetes.
   * Create a `Deployment` and a `Service` using YAML.

2. **Expose It on Browser**

   * Learn how to expose apps using `NodePort` or `LoadBalancer`.
   * Use `kubectl expose` or write a `Service` YAML.

3. **Scale Your App**

   * Increase replicas of pods.
   * Command: `kubectl scale deployment hello-deploy --replicas=3`.

4. **Delete a Pod and Watch Self-Healing**

   * Learn how Kubernetes restarts failed pods.
   * Command: `kubectl delete pod <pod-name>`.
